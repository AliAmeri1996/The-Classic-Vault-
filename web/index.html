Welcome to my website, a simple and interactive platform where you can rate and explore classic movies across four timeless genres: Crime, Fantasy, War, and Sci-Fi. As you browse through the films, you can submit your ratings and also indicate whether you’ve watched them. This helps build a better picture of which films are the most popular and highly rated in each genre. Whether you’re revisiting old favourites or discovering new classics, the platform makes it easy to see how others feel about these iconic movies and track which ones stand out as the top-rated in each category.
